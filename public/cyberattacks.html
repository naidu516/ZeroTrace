🔥 Top 10 Cyber Attacks (2020–2026)

1. SolarWinds Supply Chain Attack (2020)

  • One of the largest cyber-espionage attacks
  • Malicious code injected into software updates
  • Affected US government, Microsoft, and many organizations
  • Stayed undetected for months

2. Colonial Pipeline Ransomware Attack (2021)

  • Caused major fuel shortages in the US
  • Carried out by DarkSide ransomware group
  • Forced shutdown of a critical pipeline

3. Kaseya VSA Attack (2021)

  • Exploited IT management software
  • Affected 1500+ businesses worldwide
  • Linked to REvil ransomware

4. Costa Rica Government Attack (2022)

  • Government declared national emergency
  • Multiple systems shut down
  • Major economic disruption

5. Medibank Data Breach (2022)

  • ~10 million users affected
  • Sensitive health data leaked
  • Global privacy concerns

6. MOVEit Data Breach (2023)

  • Exploited file transfer vulnerability
  • 2700+ organizations affected
  • 90+ million users impacted

7. HTTP/2 Rapid Reset DDoS (2023)

  • ~398 million requests/sec
  • One of the largest DDoS attacks
  • Affected Google & Cloudflare

8. Change Healthcare Attack (2024)

  • Healthcare systems disrupted
  • ~6 TB data stolen
  • Hospitals & pharmacies affected

9. US Telecom Hack (2024)

  • Large-scale espionage attack
  • Access to calls and messages
  • Targeted telecom infrastructure

10. Bybit Crypto Hack (2025)

  • $1.5 billion stolen
  • Major crypto exchange breach
  • Exposed weak crypto security

⚡ Key Trends (2025–2026)

  • Rise of AI-powered cyber attacks
  • Growth of Ransomware-as-a-Service (RaaS)
  • Targeting healthcare, finance, government
